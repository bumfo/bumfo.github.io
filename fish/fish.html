# Welcome to Fish

Fish is a beautiful, flexible writing app for notes.


## Safe Everywhere

Your notes are saved automatically and that’s why there are no save button.


Fish uses /nothing/ for sync, thus all your notes are
-absolutely- safe from hackers on the _internet_. However, Fish *cannot* guarantee the security of your notes if your device itself isn’t safe.


## Reference Anywhere

Fish supports [Links](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Links_(web_browser)), `code` and more.
